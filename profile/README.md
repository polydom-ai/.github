## Our Product

### [Una](https://github.com/polydom-ai/una-by-polydom) — Autonomous AI Digital Employee

Una is an AI host for Airbnb and hotels. She answers calls, manages bookings, and handles guest requests across phone, web, WhatsApp, email, Airbnb inbox, and Booking.com — at **$1/hour**.

**What Una does:**
- 📞 Handles inbound and outbound calls with voice AI
- 💬 Communicates with guests across 12+ channels
- 📋 Creates, modifies, and cancels reservations in real time
- 💳 Collects payments via secure links
- 🛎️ Dispatches housekeeping and maintenance tasks
- 📈 Upsells rooms, services, and extras automatically
- 🌍 Speaks 90+ languages with automatic detection

**Integrated with:** Mews · Apaleo · Guesty · Hospitable · Zeevou · Clock PMS+ · Profitroom · HNext · Kommo CRM · WhatsApp · Airbnb · Booking.com
